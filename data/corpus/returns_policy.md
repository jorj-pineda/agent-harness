# Returns Policy

## Return window

Orders may be returned within 30 calendar days of the delivery date. The return window is counted from the `delivered_at` timestamp on the order, not from the order placement date. Items returned after day 30 are accepted only as a goodwill exception for premium and enterprise customers and must be approved by an escalation agent.

## Condition requirements

Returned items must be unused and in original packaging. Electronics must include all cables, manuals, and accessories. Apparel must have original tags attached. Items showing signs of use, damage, or missing components are refused at the warehouse and returned to the customer at their expense.

## Non-returnable items

Final-sale items, custom-engraved products, gift cards, and items marked "clearance" in the product catalog cannot be returned. Hygiene-related products (earbuds, headphones) are returnable only if the seal on the original packaging is unbroken.

## Refund method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Credit card refunds appear within 5–10 business days; ACH refunds within 3–5 business days. If the original card has been closed, a store credit is issued instead — customers must contact support to request a physical check, which is only offered to enterprise-tier accounts.
