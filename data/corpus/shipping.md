# Shipping and Delivery

## Shipping tiers

Standard shipping is free on orders over $50 and takes 3–5 business days. Expedited shipping is $12.99 flat and delivers in 2 business days. Overnight shipping is $29.99 and delivers next business day for orders placed before 1 PM ET. Enterprise-tier customers receive expedited shipping at no charge on every order.

## Regions

We ship to all 50 US states, Canada, and the EU. Alaska and Hawaii orders ship via standard only and add 2–3 business days. Canadian and EU orders are subject to import duties paid at delivery; we do not prepay customs.

## Order cutoffs

Orders placed before 1 PM ET on a business day ship the same day. Orders placed after 1 PM or on weekends ship the next business day. During Black Friday and December holiday windows, the cutoff moves to 11 AM ET due to carrier volume.

## Tracking

Tracking numbers are emailed once the order transitions to `shipped` status. Customers can also view tracking by looking up their order ID in the account portal. If an order has been in `shipped` status for more than 10 business days without movement, agents should file a carrier trace and offer the customer either a replacement or a refund.
