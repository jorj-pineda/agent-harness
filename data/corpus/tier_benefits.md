# Customer Tier Benefits

## Standard tier

Standard is the default tier for new accounts. It includes access to all products, standard shipping (free over $50), the 30-day returns policy, and support via ticket with a 24-hour first-response SLA. Standard customers can upgrade to premium at any time through the account portal.

## Premium tier

Premium costs $49/year and is available to any account. It adds free expedited shipping on every order, extended returns (60 days instead of 30), priority ticket handling with a 4-hour first-response SLA, and early access to sales events. Premium renews annually; cancellation takes effect at the next renewal date.

## Enterprise tier

Enterprise is provisioned by sales, not self-serve. It adds everything in premium plus: free overnight shipping, 90-day returns, a dedicated account rep, a 1-hour first-response SLA on urgent tickets, volume pricing, and invoice-based billing. Enterprise accounts require a signed agreement and are onboarded through the sales team rather than the signup flow.

## Upgrade and downgrade rules

Standard-to-premium upgrades take effect immediately and prorate any partial month. Premium-to-enterprise conversions route through the sales team. Downgrades from premium to standard take effect at the end of the current billing period; we do not prorate refunds on voluntary downgrades. Enterprise downgrades require contract review and cannot be processed by support agents.
